## Price Elasticity Reference

| Elasticity Value | Meaning | Strategy |
|-----------------|---------|----------|
| < 0.5 | Inelastic | Can increase price with minimal volume impact |
| 0.5-1.0 | Low elasticity | Small price changes have limited effect |
| 1.0-1.5 | Medium elasticity | Price sensitive, changes affect revenue |
| > 1.5 | High elasticity | Highly price sensitive, price cuts may increase revenue |

*Western markets typically show higher elasticity (1.2-1.8) for mid-tier items compared to China market (0.8-1.3)*

## Usage Notes

1. **Regional Adjustments**: North America and Europe show 1.5-2x higher price tolerance than Southeast Asia and Latin America

2. **Platform Differences**: iOS users typically show 20%-30% higher ARPPU than Android users

3. **Acquisition Channel**: Organic users show 2-3x higher LTV than paid acquisition users

4. **Lifecycle Stage**:
   - Launch Phase: Payment rate 50%-70% of mature benchmark
   - Growth Phase: Payment rate 80%-120% of mature benchmark
   - Mature Phase: Stable at benchmark
   - Decline Phase: Payment rate declines, ARPPU may increase (survivor bias)

5. **Genre Convergence**: Western casual games increasingly adopt mid-core monetization, pushing payment rates upward
